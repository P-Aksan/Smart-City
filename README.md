LIFE-CITY 2050 – AI & IoT Based Smart City System
Overview

LIFE-CITY 2050 is an Artificial Intelligence and Internet of Things based smart city prototype designed to solve modern urban challenges such as traffic congestion, pollution monitoring, water wastage, and emergency support for elderly citizens.

The system integrates multiple smart modules into a centralized platform that monitors city activities in real time and automatically performs actions based on sensor data. IoT sensors collect environmental data and send it to the cloud through an ESP32 microcontroller. A web dashboard displays the city status, and machine learning models analyze the data to predict future conditions.

Objectives

To design a centralized smart city monitoring system

To reduce water and energy wastage

To improve traffic management and accident detection

To provide emergency support for elderly and disabled citizens

To implement real-time monitoring and AI-based prediction

Project Modules
1. Smart Home Automation

Uses LDR sensors to automatically control lights based on surrounding light intensity.

2. Elder Safety & Emergency System

A push button emergency system that alerts the dashboard and activates a buzzer when pressed.

3. Smart Traffic & Accident Detection

IR sensors detect vehicles and vibration sensors detect accidents. Traffic lights are controlled automatically.

4. Smart Agriculture & Water Management

Soil moisture and water level sensors monitor soil conditions and automatically control irrigation motors.

5. AI & Machine Learning Prediction

Python-based ML models analyze collected data to predict traffic congestion and resource usage.

6. Cloud Dashboard Monitoring

A web dashboard displays real-time data such as traffic status, pollution levels, emergency alerts, and water levels.
